# Embed Proforma D/A Calculator on Your Website

You can add a fully functional **Ports Proforma D/A calculator** to your own website — 
with no visible branding, no external logos, and no indication that it is powered by 
an outside service. Your customers will feel they are using a tool that belongs entirely 
to your company.

---

## What You Get

- A fully working online Ports Proforma D/A calculator on your own website
- No external branding or logos visible to your customers
- Your customers never leave your website
- The calculator page looks and feels like part of your own site

---

## What Your Webmaster Needs To Do

1. **Create a new page** on your website
   (for example: `www.yourwebsite.com/proforma.php` or `www.yourwebsite.com/proforma.html`)

2. **Paste the following code** into that new page:

```html
    <!DOCTYPE html>
    <html>
    <head>
        <style>
            html, body {
                margin: 0;
                padding: 0;
                width: 100%;
                height: 100%;
                overflow: hidden;
            }
            iframe {
                width: 100%;
                height: 100%;
                border: none;
                display: block;
            }
        </style>
    </head>
    <body>
        <iframe src="https://www.proffora.com/membership/login.php?embed=1"
                title="Proforma Calculator">
        </iframe>
    </body>
    </html>
```

3. **Add a button or link** on your homepage or navigation menu pointing to that new page
   (for example: label it **"Proforma"**, **"D/A Calculator"**, or anything you prefer)

That is all. Your webmaster does not need any special technical knowledge beyond 
basic HTML. The whole setup takes less than 10 minutes.

---

## Managing Your Account

All account management is done through your own personal account and is completely 
separate from what your customers see on your website. Through your account you can:

- Reset your password
- Add or delete ports
- Add or delete proforma items and cost entries
- Manage all calculation settings

Your customers only see the calculator. Everything else remains private and under 
your control.

---

## Important Notes

- Your website must support HTML pages (any standard website does)
- An active account is required for the calculator to work
- The calculator works on desktop and mobile browsers

                margin: 0;
