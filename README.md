# Add a Proforma D/A Calculator to Your Website and Increase the Value of Your Agency Services

You can add a fully functional **Ports Proforma D/A calculator** to your own website.
Your customers will no longer need to wait for you to prepare a proforma — they can 
create ports proformas themselves, directly through **your website**. This way you can 
increase the quality and value of your agency services and offer your customers a modern, 
distinctive service.

No external brand name, logo, or any indication that the service is powered by an outside 
provider will be visible. Your customers will think that this proforma service is a service 
that belongs entirely to you.

---

## What You Gain

- A fully functional online Ports Proforma D/A calculator on your own website
- Customers you have shared login credentials with can create their own proformas 
  without waiting for you
- No external branding or logos visible to your customers
- Your customers never leave your website
- The proforma page looks and feels like a natural part of your own site
- The quality and value of your agency services increases

---

## What Your Webmaster Needs To Do

1. **Create a new page** on your website
   (for example: `www.yourwebsite.com/proforma.php` or `www.yourwebsite.com/proforma.html`)

2. **Paste the following code** into that new page:

```html
    <!DOCTYPE html>
    <html>
    <head>
        <style>
            html, body {
                margin: 0;
                padding: 0;
                width: 100%;
                height: 100%;
                overflow: hidden;
            }
            iframe {
                width: 100%;
                height: 100%;
                border: none;
                display: block;
            }
        </style>
    </head>
    <body>
        <iframe src="https://www.proffora.com/membership/login.php?embed=1"
                title="Proforma Calculator">
        </iframe>
    </body>
    </html>
```

3. **Add a button or link** on your homepage or navigation menu pointing to that new page
   (for example: label it **"Proforma"**, **"D/A Calculator"**, or anything you prefer)

That is all. Your webmaster does not need any special technical knowledge beyond 
basic HTML. The whole setup takes less than 10 minutes.

---

For testing: https://proffora.github.io/proforma_on_user_website/

(Note: When applied on your own website, only your web site link will be visible in the browser)

---

## Managing Your Account

All account management is done through your own personal account and is completely 
separate from what your customers see on your website. Through your account you can:

- Reset your password
- Add or delete ports
- Add or delete proforma items and cost entries
- Manage all calculation settings

Your customers only see the calculator. Everything else remains private and under 
your control.

---

## Important Notes

- Your website must support HTML pages (any standard website does)
- An active account is required for the calculator to work
- The calculator works on desktop and mobile browsers
